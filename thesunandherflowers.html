The Sun and Her Flowers

By Rupi Kaur

★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

The Sun and Her Flowers by Rupi Kaur is a heartfelt collection of poetry and prose that delves into themes of growth, healing, and self-discovery. Published in 2017, this book is organized into five sections: Wilting, Falling, Rooting, Rising, and Blooming, reflecting the journey of a flower through its life cycle and metaphorically representing the process of healing and empowerment.
