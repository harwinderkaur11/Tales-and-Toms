The Talented Mr. Ripley

A psychological thriller about Tom Ripley, a young man with a talent for impersonation and crime, who becomes embroiled in a dangerous scheme.

James Porter

The Talented Mr. Ripley is a brilliant study of identity and moral ambiguity. Ripley is one of the most fascinating characters I've ever read about, and the twists will keep you hooked.
