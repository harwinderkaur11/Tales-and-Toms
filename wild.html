Wild

By Cheryl Strayed

★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

Wild: From Lost to Found on the Pacific Crest Trail by Cheryl Strayed is a memoir that chronicles the author's 1,100-mile solo hike along the Pacific Crest Trail. At the age of 26, Strayed embarked on this journey as a way to heal from personal losses, including the death of her mother, the disintegration of her family, and her own struggles with addiction
