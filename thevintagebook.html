The Vintage Book of Contemporary American Poetry, edited by J.D. McClatchy, is a newly revised and expanded collection that brings together poems from seventy-five of America's iconic poets of the past half-century. The anthology features a diverse range of voices, from Robert Lowell and Elizabeth Bishop to John Ashbery and Adrienne Rich, to Robert Haas and Louise Glück, Sharon Olds, and Mark Doty
