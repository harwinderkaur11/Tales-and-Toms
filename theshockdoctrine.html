The Shock Doctrine: The Rise of Disaster Capitalism

By Naomi Klein

★★★★☆

Naomi Klein’s The Shock Doctrine examines the ways in which corporations and governments have used crises—wars, natural disasters, and economic collapses—to push through policies that benefit the rich and powerful at the expense of the poor and marginalized.

Price: ₹1,200

Genre: Political Science, Economics

Published: 2007

Publisher: Metropolitan Books
