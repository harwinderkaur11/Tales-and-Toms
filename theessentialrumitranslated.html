The Essential Rumi, translated by Coleman Barks, is a beautifully curated collection of the most beloved poems by the 13th-century Persian poet and Sufi mystic, Jalal ad-Din Muhammad Rumi. This edition brings together Rumi's timeless spiritual poetry, capturing themes of love, longing, and the soul's journey towards union with the divine
