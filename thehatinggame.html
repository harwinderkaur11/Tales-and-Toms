The Hating Game

Sally Thorne

★★★★☆

"The Hating Game" is a romantic comedy novel by Sally Thorne. It follows the story of Lucy Hutton and Joshua Templeman, two colleagues working at a publishing company. The two have a fierce rivalry and spend most of their time playing petty office games, each trying to outdo the other. However, when both of them are up for the same promotion, the tension between them escalates. As they engage in a battle of wills, the lines between hate and attraction begin to blur, leading to an unexpected romance. The novel is filled with witty banter, humorous situations, and heartwarming moments, making it a popular choice for fans of rom-coms.
