On Liberty

By John Stuart Mill

★★★★☆

On Liberty is a philosophical work by John Stuart Mill, first published in 1859. It discusses the nature and limits of the power that can be legitimately exercised by society over the individual. It is one of the most important texts in the liberal tradition.
