Bird Box

By Josh Malerman

★★★★★

In a post-apocalyptic world, people must navigate their surroundings blindfolded to avoid seeing deadly creatures that drive them to madness and suicide.

Price : ₹599

Genre : Horror, Thriller

Published : 2014

Publisher : Ecco
