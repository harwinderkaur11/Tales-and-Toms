The Art of War

By Sun Tzu

★★★★☆

The Art of War is an ancient Chinese military treatise attributed to Sun Tzu, a military strategist. The work is composed of 13 chapters, each dedicated to a different aspect of warfare. It has influenced military strategy, political theory, and business tactics for centuries.

Price: ₹900

Genre: Military Strategy

Published: 5th Century BC

Publisher: Various
