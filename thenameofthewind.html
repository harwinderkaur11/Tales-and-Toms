The Name of the Wind

By Patrick Rothfuss

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

The Name of the Wind is a fantasy novel by Patrick Rothfuss, published in 2007. It tells the story of Kvothe, a young man who grows up to become a legendary figure known for his extraordinary talents in music, magic, and combat. The novel is structured as a framed narrative, with an older Kvothe recounting his life story to a chronicler over the course of three days.
