A Gentleman in Moscow is a novel by Amor Towles, published in 2016. The story follows Count Alexander Ilyich Rostov, a Russian aristocrat who is sentenced to house arrest in the Metropol Hotel in Moscow by a Bolshevik tribunal in 1922.
