Me Before You

Jojo Moyes

★★★★☆

"Me Before You" is a contemporary romance novel that tells the heart-wrenching story of Louisa Clark and Will Traynor. Louisa is a quirky, cheerful young woman who becomes a caregiver for Will, a wealthy, adventurous man who has become a quadriplegic after a tragic accident. As Louisa and Will spend more time together, their bond grows, but Will is determined to end his life through assisted suicide at a clinic in Switzerland. The novel explores themes of love, disability, life choices, and the complexities of relationships.

Genre : Romance,Drama
