The Catcher in the Rye

"The Catcher in the Rye" by J.D. Salinger is a classic novel that captures the struggles of teenage angst and alienation. The story is narrated by Holden Caulfield, a 16-year-old boy who has just been expelled from an elite prep school. Over the course of a few days, Holden wanders through New York City, grappling with his feelings of disillusionment and rebellion against the phoniness he perceives in the adult world.
