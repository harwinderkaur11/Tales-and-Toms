Thinking, Fast and Slow

By Daniel Kahneman

★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

Thinking, Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman is a groundbreaking book that delves into the dual systems of thinking that drive our decisions and behaviors. Published in 2011, the book explains the two modes of thought: System 1, which is fast, intuitive, and emotional; and System 2, which is slower, more deliberate, and logical.
