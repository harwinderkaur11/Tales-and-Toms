The Book Thief by Markus Zusak is a historical novel set during World War II in Nazi Germany. The story is narrated by Death and follows a young girl named Liesel Meminger. After losing her family, Liesel is sent to live with foster parents, Hans and Rosa Hubermann, in the town of Molching.
