The Penguin Anthology of Twentieth-Century American Poetry, edited by Rita Dove, is a comprehensive collection that showcases the diversity and richness of American poetry over the past hundred years. Published in 2011, this anthology features works by renowned poets such as Wallace Stevens, T.S. Eliot, Elizabeth Bishop, Robert Hayden, Gwendolyn Brooks, Derek Walcott, Adrienne Rich, John Ashbery, Anne Sexton, and many others.
