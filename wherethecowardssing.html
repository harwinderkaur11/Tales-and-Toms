Where the Crawdads Sing

By Delia Owens

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

"Where the Crawdads Sing" by Delia Owens is a gripping novel that intertwines a coming-of-age story with a suspenseful mystery. Set in the coastal marshes of North Carolina, the novel follows Kya Clark, known as the "Marsh Girl," who grows up isolated from society after being abandoned by her family.
