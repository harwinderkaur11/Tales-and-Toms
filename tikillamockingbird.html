To Kill a Mockingbird

By Harper Lee

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

"To Kill a Mockingbird" by Harper Lee is a deeply moving and thought-provoking novel set in the racially charged atmosphere of the 1930s in the fictional town of Maycomb, Alabama. The story is narrated by Scout Finch, a young girl who recounts her experiences and observations as her father, Atticus Finch, a principled lawyer, defends a black man, Tom Robinson, falsely accused of raping a white woman.
