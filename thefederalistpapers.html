The Federalist Papers

By Alexander Hamilton, James Madison, and John Jay

★★★★☆

The Federalist Papers are a series of 85 essays arguing for the ratification of the United States Constitution. Written by Alexander Hamilton, James Madison, and John Jay, these essays explore issues of governance, federalism, and democracy, offering timeless lessons on constitutional theory.

Price: ₹1,200

Genre: Political Science

Published: 1787-1788

Publisher: Various
