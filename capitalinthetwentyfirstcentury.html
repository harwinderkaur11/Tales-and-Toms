Capital in the Twenty-First Century

By Thomas Piketty

★★★★★

In Capital in the Twenty-First Century, Thomas Piketty explores the economic disparity between the rich and poor over the past few centuries. His analysis of income inequality and wealth distribution has been widely influential and sparked global discussions.

Genre: Economics, Social Science

Published: 2013

Publisher: Harvard University Press
