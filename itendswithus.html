It ends with us

By Collen Hoover

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

"It Ends with Us" by Colleen Hoover is a contemporary romance novel that delves into the complexities of love, abuse, and resilience. The story follows Lily Bloom, a young woman who moves to Boston to open her own floral shop. She meets Ryle Kincaid, a charming neurosurgeon, and they begin a passionate but tumultuous relationship. As Lily navigates her feelings for Ryle, she is haunted by memories of her first love, Atlas Corrigan, and the abusive relationship her parents endured. The novel explores themes of domestic violence, emotional abuse, and the difficult choices one must make to break free from the cycle of abuse.
