The Soul of America: The Battle for Our Better Angels

By Jon Meacham

★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

The Soul of America: The Battle for Our Better Angels by Jon Meacham is a thought-provoking exploration of the history of the United States, focusing on the periods of intense social and political turmoil. Meacham examines how the nation has faced and overcome challenges such as racial injustice, nativism, and political division.
