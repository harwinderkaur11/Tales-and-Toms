Becoming

By Michelle Obama

★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

Becoming by Michelle Obama is a deeply personal memoir that chronicles her journey from a modest upbringing on the South Side of Chicago to becoming the First Lady of the United States. Michelle Obama shares her experiences growing up in a close-knit family, her education at Princeton University and Harvard Law School, and her early career as a lawyer.
