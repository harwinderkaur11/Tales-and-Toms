Why Nations Fail: The Origins of Power, Prosperity, and Poverty

By Daron Acemoglu and James A. Robinson

★★★★★

In this groundbreaking book, Acemoglu and Robinson explore the political and economic institutions that have shaped the fate of nations. They argue that inclusive institutions lead to prosperity, while extractive institutions lead to failure.

Price: ₹1,300

Genre: Economics, Political Science

Published: 2012

Publisher: Crown Publishing
