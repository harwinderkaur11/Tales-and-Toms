The Body Keeps the Score: Brain, Mind, and Body in the Healing of Trauma by Bessel van der Kolk is a groundbreaking book that delves into the effects of psychological trauma on the mind and body. Published in 2014, it explores how trauma can reshape both brain and body, affecting individuals' capacities for pleasure, engagement, self-control, and trust
