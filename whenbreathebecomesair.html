When Breath Becomes Air

When Breath Becomes Air by Paul Kalanithi is a deeply moving memoir that chronicles the author's journey from a neurosurgeon to a patient diagnosed with stage IV lung cancer. Published posthumously in 2016, the book explores profound questions about life, death, and what makes a life meaningful
