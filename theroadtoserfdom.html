The Road to Serfdom

By Friedrich Hayek

★★★★★

Hayek’s The Road to Serfdom warns against the dangers of collectivism and central planning, arguing that they lead to totalitarianism. The book is a classic defense of classical liberalism, individual freedom, and free-market capitalism.

Price: ₹1,000

Genre: Political Science

Published: 1944

Publisher: University of Chicago Press





Reviews

Michael Peters:

An essential work for understanding the dangers of excessive state power. Hayek’s arguments are still relevant today, making this a must-read for modern political thinkers.

Olivia Thompson:

Fascinating and insightful. Hayek’s critique of socialism and his defense of individual liberty is both timeless and persuasive.
