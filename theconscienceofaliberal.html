The Conscience of a Liberal

By Paul Krugman

★★★★☆

In this book, economist Paul Krugman argues that the increasing inequality in American society has been driven by policy choices, particularly since the 1970s. He explores the relationship between economic and political systems, making the case for liberal values.

Price: ₹1,100

Genre: Economics, Political Science

Published: 2007

Publisher: W.W. Norton & Company





Reviews

Richard Lee:

Krugman’s analysis of economic inequality is deeply compelling and thought-provoking. A crucial read for anyone concerned with the future of the American economy.
