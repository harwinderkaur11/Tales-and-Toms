The Night Circus

By Erin Morgenstern

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

"The Night Circus" by Erin Morgenstern is a mesmerizing fantasy novel set in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. The story revolves around Le Cirque des Rêves, a magical circus that appears without warning and is only open at night. The circus is filled with fantastical and surreal wonders that captivate its visitors.
